Recipes

  • 1. Quinoa Salad with Chicken
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 cup cooked quinoa
      • 1 grilled chicken breast (sliced)
      • 1 cup steamed broccoli
      • 2 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tsp lemon juice
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Combine quinoa, broccoli, and chicken in a bowl.
      • Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 2. Stir-Fried Beef with Vegetables
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 lb beef sirloin (sliced thinly)
      • 1 zucchini (sliced)
      • 2 bell peppers (sliced)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
      • 1 tsp sesame seeds
    • Instructions:
      • Sauté beef in olive oil until browned.
      • Add vegetables and cook until tender.
      • Drizzle with soy sauce and sprinkle sesame seeds.
  • 3. Grilled Salmon with Roasted Sweet Potatoes
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 salmon fillets
      • 2 medium sweet potatoes (cubed)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • Salt, pepper, and rosemary to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 400°F. Toss sweet potatoes in olive oil and rosemary. Roast for 30 minutes.
      • Grill salmon for 5-6 minutes on each side. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 4. Baked Chicken with Sautéed Broccoli
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 chicken breasts
      • 2 cups broccoli (sautéed)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
      • Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until fully cooked.
      • Sauté broccoli in olive oil for 5-6 minutes and serve.
  • 5. Avocado Toast with Grapefruit
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 slice whole grain bread (toasted)
      • 1/2 avocado (mashed)
      • 1 grapefruit (on the side)
    • Instructions:
      • Spread avocado on toasted bread.
      • Serve with a side of grapefruit.
  • 6. Spinach and Mushroom Frittata
    • Ingredients:
      • 6 eggs
      • 1 cup spinach
      • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
      • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Sauté mushrooms in olive oil until soft.
      • Add spinach and cook until wilted.
      • Whisk eggs and cheese together, then add to the skillet.
      • Bake for 20 minutes or until set.
  • 7. Turkey and Veggie Lettuce Wraps
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 lb ground turkey
      • 1 bell pepper (diced)
      • 1 zucchini (diced)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • Lettuce leaves (for wrapping)
    • Instructions:
      • Cook turkey in olive oil until browned.
      • Add bell pepper and zucchini, and cook until tender.
      • Serve in lettuce leaves.
  • 8. Cauliflower Rice Stir-Fry
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 head cauliflower (grated into rice-sized pieces)
      • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas)
      • 1 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Sauté vegetables in olive oil until tender.
      • Add cauliflower rice and soy sauce, and cook for 5 minutes.
  • 9. Greek Salad with Chicken
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 grilled chicken breast (sliced)
      • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
      • 1/2 cucumber (sliced)
      • 1/4 cup feta cheese
      • 2 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tbsp lemon juice
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Combine all ingredients in a bowl and toss with olive oil and lemon juice.
  • 10. Zucchini Noodles with Pesto
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 zucchinis (spiralized)
      • 1/4 cup pesto
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Sauté zucchini noodles in olive oil until tender.
      • Mix with pesto and serve.
  • 11. Baked Cod with Lemon and Herbs
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 cod fillets
      • 1 lemon (sliced)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tsp dried herbs (thyme, oregano)
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Place cod fillets on a baking sheet.
      • Drizzle with olive oil and top with lemon slices and herbs.
      • Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  • 12. Eggplant Parmesan
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 eggplant (sliced)
      • 1 cup marinara sauce (sugar-free)
      • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
      • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and bake for 20 minutes.
      • Top with marinara sauce and cheeses, then bake for an additional 15 minutes.

30-Day Keto Shopping List

  • Proteins: 4 chicken breasts, 1 lb beef sirloin, 2 salmon fillets, 1 lb ground turkey, 1 lb cod, 1 dozen eggs
  • Healthy Fats: 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp sesame seeds, 1/4 cup feta cheese, 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, 1/4 cup pesto
  • Dairy (optional): 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese (for eggplant Parmesan)
