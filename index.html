Recipes

  • 1. Quinoa Salad with Chicken
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 cup cooked quinoa
      • 1 grilled chicken breast (sliced)
      • 1 cup steamed broccoli
      • 2 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tsp lemon juice
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Combine quinoa, broccoli, and chicken in a bowl.
      • Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 2. Stir-Fried Tofu with Vegetables
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 block firm tofu (cubed)
      • 1 zucchini (sliced)
      • 2 carrots (sliced)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
      • 1 tsp sesame seeds
    • Instructions:
      • Sauté tofu in olive oil until browned.
      • Add vegetables and cook until tender.
      • Drizzle with soy sauce and sprinkle sesame seeds.
  • 3. Grilled Salmon with Roasted Sweet Potatoes
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 salmon fillets
      • 2 medium sweet potatoes (cubed)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • Salt, pepper, and rosemary to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 400°F. Toss sweet potatoes in olive oil and rosemary. Roast for 30 minutes.
      • Grill salmon for 5-6 minutes on each side. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 4. Baked Chicken with Sautéed Broccoli
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 chicken breasts
      • 2 cups broccoli (sautéed)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
      • Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until fully cooked.
      • Sauté broccoli in olive oil for 5-6 minutes and serve.
  • 5. Avocado Toast with Grapefruit
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 slice whole grain bread (toasted)
      • 1/2 avocado (mashed)
      • 1 grapefruit (on the side)
    • Instructions:
      • Spread avocado on toasted bread.
      • Serve with a side of grapefruit.
  • 6. Spinach and Mushroom Frittata
    • Ingredients:
      • 6 eggs
      • 1 cup spinach
      • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
      • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Sauté mushrooms in olive oil until soft.
      • Add spinach and cook until wilted.
      • Whisk eggs and cheese together, then add to the skillet.
      • Bake for 20 minutes or until set.
  • 7. Turkey and Veggie Lettuce Wraps
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 lb ground turkey
      • 1 bell pepper (diced)
      • 1 zucchini (diced)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • Lettuce leaves (for wrapping)
    • Instructions:
      • Cook turkey in olive oil until browned.
      • Add bell pepper and zucchini, and cook until tender.
      • Serve in lettuce leaves.
  • 8. Cauliflower Rice Stir-Fry
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 head cauliflower (grated into rice-sized pieces)
      • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
      • 1 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Sauté vegetables in olive oil until tender.
      • Add cauliflower rice and soy sauce, and cook for 5 minutes.
  • 9. Greek Salad with Chicken
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 grilled chicken breast (sliced)
      • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
      • 1/2 cucumber (sliced)
      • 1/4 cup feta cheese
      • 2 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tbsp lemon juice
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Combine all ingredients in a bowl and toss with olive oil and lemon juice.
  • 10. Zucchini Noodles with Pesto
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 zucchinis (spiralized)
      • 1/4 cup pesto
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Sauté zucchini noodles in olive oil until tender.
      • Mix with pesto and serve.
  • 11. Baked Cod with Lemon and Herbs
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 cod fillets
      • 1 lemon (sliced)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tsp dried herbs (thyme, oregano)
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Place cod fillets on a baking sheet.
      • Drizzle with olive oil and top with lemon slices and herbs.
      • Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  • 12. Eggplant Parmesan
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 eggplant (sliced)
      • 1 cup marinara sauce (sugar-free)
      • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
      • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and bake for 20 minutes.
      • Top with marinara sauce and cheeses, and bake for another 10 minutes.
  • 13. Chicken and Avocado Salad
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 grilled chicken breast (sliced)
      • 1 avocado (diced)
      • 1 cup mixed greens
      • 2 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • Instructions:
      • Combine all ingredients in a bowl and toss with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  • 14. Shrimp and Avocado Lettuce Wraps
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 lb shrimp (peeled and deveined)
      • 1 avocado (sliced)
      • Lettuce leaves (for wrapping)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tbsp lime juice
    • Instructions:
      • Cook shrimp in olive oil until pink.
      • Serve in lettuce leaves with avocado slices and lime juice.
  • 15. Almond-Crusted Chicken Tenders
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 lb chicken tenders
      • 1 cup almond flour
      • 1 egg (beaten)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Dip chicken tenders in egg, then coat with almond flour.
      • Place on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes.
  • 16. Cucumber and Hummus Bites
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 cucumber (sliced)
      • 1/2 cup hummus (check for no added sugars)
    • Instructions:
      • Spread hummus on cucumber slices and serve.
  • 17. Balsamic Glazed Brussels Sprouts
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 cups Brussels sprouts (halved)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 400°F. Toss Brussels sprouts in olive oil and bake for 20 minutes.
      • Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and serve.
  • 18. Broccoli and Cheddar Stuffed Chicken
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 chicken breasts
      • 1 cup broccoli (steamed and chopped)
      • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Stuff chicken breasts with broccoli and cheddar.
      • Season with salt and pepper, then bake for 25-30 minutes.
  • 19. Avocado and Egg Salad
    • Ingredients:
      • 4 hard-boiled eggs (chopped)
      • 1 avocado (diced)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Combine eggs and avocado in a bowl.
      • Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  • 20. Grilled Portobello Mushrooms
    • Ingredients:
      • 4 Portobello mushrooms
      • 2 tbsp olive oil
      • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
      • 1 tsp dried thyme
    • Instructions:
      • Brush mushrooms with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
      • Season with thyme and grill for 5-7 minutes per side.
  • 21. Kale and Chicken Soup
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 cups chicken broth
      • 1 cup kale (chopped)
      • 1 chicken breast (cooked and shredded)
      • 1 carrot (diced)
      • 1 celery stalk (diced)
    • Instructions:
      • Combine all ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil.
      • Simmer for 20 minutes, then serve.
  • 22. Creamy Avocado Soup
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 avocados
      • 1 cup vegetable broth
      • 1/2 cup coconut milk
      • 1 lime (juiced)
      • Salt to taste
    • Instructions:
      • Blend all ingredients until smooth. Chill before serving.
  • 23. Spaghetti Squash with Marinara Sauce
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 spaghetti squash
      • 1 cup marinara sauce (sugar-free)
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 400°F. Cut spaghetti squash in half and remove seeds.
      • Drizzle with olive oil and bake for 40 minutes.
      • Scrape out strands and top with marinara sauce.
  • 24. Stuffed Bell Peppers
    • Ingredients:
      • 4 bell peppers
      • 1 lb ground beef
      • 1 cup cauliflower rice
      • 1 cup diced tomatoes
      • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Instructions:
      • Preheat oven to 375°F. Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds.
      • Cook ground beef in olive oil, then mix with cauliflower rice and tomatoes.
      • Stuff bell peppers with the mixture and bake for 30 minutes.
  • 25. Greek Yogurt Parfait
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
      • 1/4 cup nuts (almonds, walnuts)
      • 1/4 cup berries (raspberries or blackberries)
    • Instructions:
      • Layer Greek yogurt with nuts and berries in a glass.
